BUY NOW BUY NOW

DESCRIPTION

Firaga Records teams up with multi-genre net-label Novatone, bringing the first volume of the VGM Genre Series - VGM Essentials: House. A collection of 15 dance floor ready house remixes from video games such as Pokemon, Sonic, Super Mario, Chrono Trigger, Kirby, Hollow Knight, Yume Nikki, and more!

With remixes by floopy, RoboRob & DeBisco, Arcarine, NoteBlock, Wontolla, RoBKTA, PrototypeRaptor, Theology & DJ Dark Prime, nokbient, Galaxian, and more - this album showcases a variety of house genres from Electro, Tech House, Bass House, and Disco! VGM Essentials: House is a must listen for gamers and music fans alike!
