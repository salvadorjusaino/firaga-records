ROBKA

PRODUCER,

REMIXER

  • BIO

    RoB | Italian transplanted in the UK | he/they |

    Racing Game Junkie, Professional Ridge Racer, Proficient in Disco, House, and a blend of 90s fast beats and jazzy grooves that he calls Neo-Arcade

    RoBKTA released music through various channels and labels, including GameChops, DESKPOP and Tiny Waves, as well as finding fertile ground for soundtrack work for videogames like appearing in the Switch port for Crypt Of Necrodancer, scoring the mobile game Super Leap Day and currently working on the upcoming racing game Victory Heat Rally.
